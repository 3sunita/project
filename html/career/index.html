CAREER CONSULTING

Are you still searching for your dream job?
A recent graduate and still looking for your first job?
A recent migrant and are unable to get into the job market?
A working professional and struggling for your next role?

Working but want to change your career?
Made redundant and looking for your next role?
Working but don’t love your work?


If you are facing any of the above listed difficulties, then you have landed at the right place.

With a proven track record of 10 years, we are helping people in finding a job they dream of. However, while applying for a job you should know there might be hundreds of job-seekers competing for the same role but candidates with the most effective job searching strategies will make a difference. Securing a job is not enough, you will have to demonstrate certain skills like interpersonal skills, being culturally fit, right attitude which are becoming mandatory to achieve success in today’s fast paced work environment. At Education Xplorer, we will align a personal career coach with you who will prepare your professional portfolio, help you in unleashing the hidden job market, and make you job ready.

How does Education Xplorer help Graduates?
After studying for years and gaining all the certifications required for your respective field, you will be still facing difficulty in “getting your foot into the door” for your first job and the situation becomes more critical when you are unable to get the job as quickly as you thought you would get. Often, graduates lack the skills required by the highly competitive job market and fail to make their first impression in an interview. We will help in preparing you for making your first impression in an interview and getting your dream job.
How does Education Xplorer help Migrants?
Often highly qualified overseas professionals move to Australia looking for a better opportunity and to fulfil their dream of working in a new fast paced environment. However, they often face extreme difficulty in finding a job even after applying for hundreds of jobs and end up with job which is not of their interest. Unfortunately, migrants have very less understanding of the local job market and different job seeking strategies which can give them a better edge as compared to other job seekers. We will help you in understanding the market trends and will make sure you stand out amongst other individuals who are struggling for a job.
