ABOUT EDUCATION XPLORER

OUR PROFILE & HISTORY

Education Xplorer's mission is to be the best provider of technical and application training globally. We provide the most outstanding instruction, staff support, facilities, testing centers in the country. We offer one of the largest offerings of training programs, the most difficult technical classes, and offer all inclusive packages for the ease of our students and hands-on instruction, course materials, practice exams, and certification exams while providing the highest rate of satisfaction and achievement possible by our students.

Education Xplorer provides the most thorough training available on over multiple programs throughout the country and the world. We train thousands of students each year on Cisco, Microsoft, Linux/Unix, and more. Education Xplorer is a CISCO Partner and all CISCO instructors are certified CISCO instructors and certified in the courses they teach.

We have been in business for more than 10 years. We currently provide IT training for more than 10,000 students per year. We provide training for individuals and some of the largest corporations, governmental agencies, and organizations in INDIA. Our instructors are some of the very best in the industry and are certified in the areas of their expertise. Our instructors are concerned about your success and will do whatever is necessary to help you be successful in your studies.

Education Xplorer provides nationwide IT training. Education Xplorer is your one-stop shop for IT training, from everything from the popular CCNA to CCIE, and MICROSOFT training.

Our nationwide technical training schedule are full of the most popular cutting-edge classes, with a special emphasis on all CISCO tracks boot camps and certification, Microsoft training, and linux training -- because you asked for it.

Our certified instructors teach classes at numerous locations nationwide, and we also offer the option of dedicated classes, on-site IT training at your facility, room rentals, and even technical training courses that are not listed on our Boot Camp Training Schedule...all you have to do is ask, and we will strive to meet your needs! We can also direct you to financing for our very popular CCIE training!
