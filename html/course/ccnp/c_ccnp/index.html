ccnp collaboration

Introduction

The CCNP Collaboration course is designed for network engineers who want to develop advanced collaboration skills in deploying, designing, configuring, troubleshooting of enterprise level Cisco networks. The certification course is entirely job-role focused which enables the individual to manage network servers for optimal performance.

A valid CCNA Collaboration or any other CCIE certification can act as a pre-requisite. We prepare students for the CCNA Collaboration course extensively before taking this course.

Why take this course?

The CCNP Collaboration certification course helps an individual in

  • Advanced collaboration in unified communications

  • Collaboration and communication over small medium to large enterprise environment.

  • Logging calls directly with Cisco TAC for advanced support.

  • Advanced approach in troubleshooting, deployment and configuration of

    Cisco network structures.

Pre-requisites

    A valid CCNA Collaboration certification or any Cisco CCIE can act as a pre-requisite for this course.

Skills Gained

  • Advanced voice and video network engineering.

  • Advanced support in issues related to video and network.

  • Endpoint configuration in voice and telepresence.

  • Manage Cisco Unified Communications System.

Audience

  • CCNA Collaboration certified Voice and video engineers looking to expand their knowledge

    in collaborative architecture.

  • CCNA Collaboration certified Network and System engineers looking to advance in

    their career with the skills high in demand.
