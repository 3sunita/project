ccna routing & switching

Introduction

The CCNA Routing and Switching certification prepares an individual with the foundation knowledge in operating and configuring LANs and WANs. Candidates can choose to take either of the two exams viz. ICND1 100-101 and ICND2 200-101 or the composite exam 200-120.

We at educationXplorer provide in depth knowledge to candidates through our expert instructors so that candidates face no difficulty in getting through CCENT, a prerequisite to all or most Cisco certifications which is the entry level certification covering the ICND1 100-101 exam.

The CCNA Routing and Switching certified professional can install, operate, configure and troubleshoot small and medium sized routed and switched networks. Implementation of IPV4 and IPV6, default routes, EIGRP and Open Shortest Path First (OSPF v2 and v3) are some of the tasks which the professional can handle.

A CCNA Routing and Switching trained professional can expand his / her skills by attaining further certifications in the CCNA domain such as the CCNA Security, CCNA Collaboration and CCNA Wireless.
